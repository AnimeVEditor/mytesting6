Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Flavours

Welcome to Punjabee Rasoi, where every dish tells a story of rich heritage and culinary passion. Located in the heart of Kolkata, we bring the vibrant, robust, and heartwarming flavors of authentic North Indian and Punjabi cuisine straight to your table.

From our signature slow-cooked Dal Makhani to our perfectly charred Tandoori specialties, every recipe is crafted using fresh, premium ingredients and traditional cooking methods. Experience warm hospitality in a setting designed for family dining and unforgettable moments.
